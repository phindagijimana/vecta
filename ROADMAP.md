# Development Roadmap

## Phase 1: Neurology & Neuroscience (Current)

**Focus Areas:**
- Epilepsy classification and seizure analysis
- Movement disorders (Parkinson's, tremors, ataxia)
- Cognitive assessment (dementia, MCI)
- Stroke and cerebrovascular disease
- Neurodegenerative diseases (Alzheimer's, MS)
- Headache classification
- Neurological medication optimization

**Status:** Active Development

## Phase 2: Expansion (Future)

**Planned Specialties:**
- Cardiology
- Psychiatry
- Emergency Medicine
- Internal Medicine

**Implementation:** Code structure ready, will be activated based on user demand and validation.

**Current Version:** 2.0 (Neurology-Focused)
